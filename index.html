MISSION STATEMENT

LAUNCH is a student-run organization that provides a community of interdisciplinary learners with a foundation in technology. We focus on two areas: educational events and collaborative projects. Our events are geared towards educating students from all backgrounds on how they can use technology to achieve their innovative ideas while our projects provide them with opportunities to apply their newly learned skill-sets. Ultimately, LAUNCH equips students with relevant skills and experience so that they can lead and forge their own paths after graduation.
